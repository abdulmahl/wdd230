Typography

On all my headings I'm using "Ubuntu" which is a google font API, and on all the my paragraphs and the rest of my elements I have applied "Rajdhani". Both these fonts are backed by "sans-serif" a font that is supported by most machines and operating systems, just in case my main fonts are not supported.
