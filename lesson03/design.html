Typography

Heading Font: "Ubuntu"
Paragrph Font: "Rajdhani"
On all my headings I'm using "Ubuntu" which is a google font API, and on all the my paragraphs and the rest of my elements I have applied "Rajdhani". Both these fonts are backed by "sans-serif" a font that is supported by most machines and operating systems, just in case my main fonts are not supported.

Color Schema

The color schemes that I chose to apply are Lavender for all my headers and footers (primary). I then applied Raisin Black for my navigation bar (Secondary), I also applied Lavender for all the header background colors, font color that I used for my last updated on my footer is Davy's Gray. The colors on the logo came as from where I made the logo, canva.com.
