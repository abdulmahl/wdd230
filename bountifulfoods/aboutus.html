About Us

Once upon a time, two friends named John and Lisa were passionate about living a healthy lifestyle and incorporating fresh, natural ingredients into their diet. They noticed a gap in the market for a local juice bar that offered truly fresh and organic options, so they decided to take matters into their own hands. In 2010, John and Lisa pooled their savings and opened a small juice bar in Carlsbad, California. They sourced the freshest fruits and vegetables from local farms and created unique juice blends that were both delicious and nutritious.

The response from the community was overwhelming, and their juice bar quickly became a local favorite. As their business grew, John and Lisa decided to expand their offerings to include smoothies, acai bowls, and other healthy snacks. They also made a commitment to sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging and working with local organizations to reduce food waste. Now, over a decade later, John and Lisa's juice bar has become a staple in the Carlsbad community. They have multiple locations throughout the city and a loyal following of customers who appreciate their commitment to health, sustainability, and supporting the local community.
